Intermediate

The Pre-University Course or Pre-Degree Course (PUC or PDC) is an Intermediate Course (which is known as 10+2) of two years' duration, refers to Class 11th and Class 12th and called as 1st PUC and 2nd PUC respectively in the PU Colleges or Junior Colleges and are conducted by state education institutions or boards in india.

Degree

The Pre-University Course or Pre-Degree Course (PUC or PDC) is an Intermediate Course (which is known as 12+3) of three years' duration, refers to Class 13th and Class 14th and called as 1st PUC and 2nd PUC respectively in the PU Colleges or Junior Colleges and are conducted by state education institutions or boards in india.

Post Graduation

The Pre-University Course or Pre-Degree Course (PUC or PDC) is an Intermediate Course (which is known as 12+4) of two years' duration, refers to Class 11th and Class 12th and called as 1st PUC and 2nd PUC respectively in the PU Colleges or Junior Colleges and are conducted by state education institutions or boards in india.
